Jay Chooi

I work on preparing society for powerful artificial intelligence.

Currently, I am a Research Fellow at MATS building detections on covert communication between AI models. Outside of technical AI safety, I am interested in measuring societal disempowerment and extreme power concentration due to AI. I welcome collaborations in this direction, so feel free to reach out.

In May, I will graduate from Harvard with my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Mathematics, and a Master's in Statistics. I am a Rhodes Scholar for 2025.
