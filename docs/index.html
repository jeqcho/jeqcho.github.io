Jay Chooi

I work on preparing society for powerful artificial intelligence.

Currently, I am a Research Fellow at MATS working on technical AI safety research. More broadly, my research interests are in forecasting physical automation and how society can remain stable and flourish in a post-automation world.

In May 2026, I will graduate from Harvard with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Mathematics, and a Master's in Statistics. I am a Rhodes Scholar for 2026 and a gold medalist at the 2021 International Olympiad in Astronomy and Astrophysics.
