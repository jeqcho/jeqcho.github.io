Jay Chooi

I work on preparing society for powerful artificial intelligence.

Currently, I am a Research Fellow at MATS. My research interests are forecasting physical automation, and understanding how society can remain stable and flourish when political power and economic leverage from human labor diminish.

I graduated from Harvard with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Mathematics, with a concurrent Master's in Statistics. There, I was fortunate to be mentored by Professor Ariel Procaccia. My thesis, Computational Perspectives on Democracy in the Age of AI, was awarded Highest Honors.

I am a Rhodes Scholar for 2026. In 2024, my US Presidential Election forecast got all 50 states correct. I am also a gold medalist at the 2021 International Olympiad in Astronomy and Astrophysics.
